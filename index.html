About Me

I'm an experienced Instructional Designer & eLearning Developer with over eight years of expertise in creating interactive, learner-centered training solutions. My work combines solid instructional design foundations—ADDIE, SAM, and Kirkpatrick's models—with technical proficiency in a wide range of authoring tools, including Articulate Storyline, Adobe Captivate, Xyleme, Camtasia, and the Adobe Creative Cloud Suite.

I specialize in building SCORM-compliant modules for LMS integration, transforming complex concepts into engaging digital learning experiences. Whether collaborating with cross-functional teams or developing courses independently, I bring both creative design thinking and technical precision to every project.

Passionate about improving learning outcomes through thoughtful design, I invite you to explore my portfolio and see how I turn instructional goals into impactful eLearning.
